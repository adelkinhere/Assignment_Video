About the Film

"Prom?" is an interactive short film that explores the pivotal moments of teenage life and the decisions that shape our future. Set against the backdrop of high school prom season, our protagonist faces choices that might seem small in the moment but have lasting consequences.

This project was created as an experimental narrative that allows viewers to step into the shoes of our main character and make decisions that alter the storyline. Inspired by modern interactive cinema, we wanted to blur the lines between film and audience participation.

The film was shot over two weekends with a dedicated student crew, using innovative techniques to create a seamless interactive experience. We hope you enjoy the journey and explore all possible paths and endings.
